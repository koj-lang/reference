## Keywords

The following keywords are reserved by the `koj` language:

- KW_AS: `as`
- KW_BREAK: `break`
- KW_CATCH: `catch`
- KW_CONST: `const`
- KW_CONTINUE: `continue`
- KW_DO: `do`
- KW_EACH: `each`
- KW_ELSE: `else`
- KW_ENUM: `enum`
- KW_FALSE: `false`
- KW_FOR: `for`
- KW_FUNC: `func`
- KW_IF: `if`
- KW_IS: `is`
- KW_LET: `let`
- KW_MATCH: `match`
- KW_MUT: `mut`
- KW_RETURN: `return`
- KW_STRUCT: `struct`
- KW_THROW: `throw`
- KW_TRUE: `true`
- KW_TRY: `try`
- KW_TYPE: `type`
- KW_UNION: `union`
- KW_WHILE: `while`

This list may change as the language evolves.

There keywords are reserved and cannot be used as identifiers.
